Project 1: Digital Control Logic

  • Category: PLC Programming
  • Software: RSLogix Micro Starter Lite, RSLinx Classic Lite, RSLogix Emulate 500
  • Course: PLCDojo.com > Applied Logic (Level 2)
  • Project date: 03 October, 2024
  • Project PDF: Project 1-Digital Control Logic PROGRAM FILES.pdf

Digital Control Logic for a Pressure Tank Application

  • Customer specification is to maintain pressure in a tank between 90 and 110 PSI.
  • Tank will use 2 Digital Pressure Switches as well as a Compressor.
  • Pressure Switch 1 turns on at 90 PSI and Pressure Switch 2 turns on a 110 PSI.
  • An indicator light will also turn on when Pressure is above 90 PSI to indicate that work tool is ready for use.
